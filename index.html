Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AnviPayz

What is AnviPayz?

AnviPayz is a premium, free-to-use online rewards platform in India. Users can earn reward points by completing simple daily tasks, micro-surveys, and inviting friends. These reward points can be converted into tokens and redeemed for mobile recharge discounts across all major mobile operators.

How do I earn rewards?

You can easily earn reward points in multiple ways: complete quick daily tasks and surveys on your dashboard, participate in the daily spin wheel, and invite friends using your unique referral rewards link.

How does the referral system work?

When you invite friends using your personal referral link, they sign up for free. Once they verify their email using the secure OTP, you both receive bonus reward points credited directly to your wallets.

How do recharge benefits work?

As you accumulate reward points, you can convert them into tokens instantly inside your wallet. When you initiate a mobile recharge on our platform, you can apply your available tokens to get significant recharge discounts off your total recharge amount.

Is AnviPayz free to join?

Yes, AnviPayz is 100% free to join. There are absolutely no hidden registration fees, monthly subscriptions, or charges to convert points and redeem your mobile recharge benefits.

How long does OTP verification take?

OTP verification is instant. The one-time password is sent to your email immediately upon clicking register or login, allowing you to access your dashboard securely in under a minute.

Can I use AnviPayz on mobile?

Absolutely! AnviPayz is fully mobile-optimized. The dashboard, tasks, wallet, and recharge portal are built with responsive layouts that scale perfectly on all screen sizes, from small smartphones to tablets.

Is my account secure?

Yes, your account is highly secure. We use secure email OTP verification to prevent unauthorized access, enforce active security guards like device and IP level verification limits, and use SSL encryption to safeguard all your data.
