Our Mission

Our Story

In an increasingly digital India, mobile connectivity is not a luxury—it is a necessity for education, work, and communication. However, with rising mobile tariff plans, maintaining continuous mobile data has become a monthly financial burden for millions of students and daily-wage earners.

AnviPayz started with a simple question: How can we empower users to subsidize their own mobile connectivity costs using their spare time?

Our team built a platform that translates daily micro-tasks, feedback surveys, and game modules into real, tangible monetary savings on recharges. We bypass complex points schemas with a direct, transparent utility token system.

Our Vision: To democratize internet access across India, ensuring no student or worker is ever cut off from opportunities due to recharge constraints.

FAQ

About FAQ

How does AnviPayz subsidize recharges?
We partner with advertising platforms and research agencies (like CPX Research). When you complete tasks or surveys, these partners pay us. We pass the majority of this payout directly back to you as reward points.
What is the conversion value of tokens?
Every 1,000 points converts into 10 AnviPayz Utility Tokens. 1 Token equals a direct ₹1 discount reduction on any mobile recharge plan checkout.
Are there any charges for converting tokens?
No. Conversion from points to tokens is completely free. We do not deduct any processing fees or maintenance overheads.
