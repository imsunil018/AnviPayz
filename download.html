FAQ

Common Questions

Is this available on Play Store?
No, it is distributed directly from our website as a Progressive Web App (PWA). This saves memory space on your phone and lets us deliver instant reward updates without store delay.
Is it safe?
Yes, AnviPayz is 100% safe. It uses standard secure HTTPS protocol, safe JWT login credentials, and runs securely within your native browser sandbox with zero access to system files.
Does it work offline?
Core pages and elements load offline thanks to the service worker cache. However, completing tasks, surveys, spins, and recharges requires a working internet connection.
How do I update the app?
You do not need to update it manually. Because AnviPayz is a PWA, it updates automatically in the background every time you open it.
Can I uninstall anytime?
Yes. You can uninstall it just like a regular native app. Long-press the AnviPayz icon on your mobile screen and tap "Uninstall" or "Remove App".
